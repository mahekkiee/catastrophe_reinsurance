🛡️ Catastrophic Loss Clustering

Optimal Reinsurance Trigger Design Using Markov Chains & Monte Carlo

📊 Project Overview

🎯 The Problem

Indian insurers use static reinsurance triggers ignoring catastrophe clustering. When one disaster hits (2015, 2018, 2021), more claims follow within years.

💡 Our Solution

Markov regime-switching model captures clustering pattern. Validated with IRDAI real data. Identifies optimal 800 Cr trigger scientifically.

📈 Impact

3-5% cost savings industry-wide. Applicable to all 58 Indian insurance companies. Data-driven decision making.

Key Insight: Catastrophic years show 3.7x more claims than normal years, creating clustering pattern perfectly captured by Markov chains with 2 states (Normal/Catastrophic).
